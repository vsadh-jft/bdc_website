THE SITUATION

The client is a renowned market research firm. Working with them, Business D Consulting Accepted and signed a project observing the following structure of the Analytics Department at day 1.

As A Result Of The Workflow organised this way:

- Each division could have produced 2 dashboards per client every month (each division manage one client).
- Communication issues having involved professionals of different backgrounds connected by a dependency relationship
- Frequent Issues in data validation which reduced overall trust in the value provided by data
- Poor process automation with hours spent every month in data manipulation and data validation
- Frequent Stops to the projects in each division due to dependency by central data system extraction
- Issues in respecting the deadlines
- Overall a reporting system presenting graphic limitations a due to use of excel for reporting.

OUR SOLUTION

As We Observed the structure we proposed to keep the central data warehouse, avoid the central release of data by sql experts and instead train the excel experts to use Alteryx and Tableau to extract data from the central data warehouse and to visualise insights in tableau without any dependency end to end. We Decided also to keep the account manager given his business background to present data to the client.

The Project Had a Duration of six month with involvement of 5 of business d consulting experts:

1 Project Manager
2 Tableau and Alteryx consultant, specialised in delivering end to end automated business intelligence solutions
3 tableau and alteryx trainers, specialised in delivering classroom training within any organisation

HOW WE SET UP THE PROJECT

During The Six Month We Created Two Team Managed By A Project Manager


Team A : Two External Tableau And Alteryx Consultants to Restructure the reporting system along the 5 divisions while Excel experts were involved in training. These Consultants restructured the 2 reports per division liaising end to end with the account manager and the database administrators.

Team B : Two External Trainers To teach Alteryx, Tableau And Project management skills to the 5 excel experts and the sql expert. The training was organised to be 2 h per day everyday: 1h classroom + 1h Consultantâ€™s live experience. This obviously allow experts to maintain normal working operations.

THE RESULTING STRUCTURE

At The end of the project we placed the 5 alteryx and tableau consultants back to their divisions. Each Consultant had in place the previous 2 dashboards now automatic (developed in the meantime by the external business d consulting experts) so they can focus on the development of new relevant dashboards while improving the existing ones. The sql expert re-trained now could be used to acquire a new client and create a new division.

THE VALUE WE ADDED

We Reduced the communication issues , completely avoiding one step of the original structure : The involvement of an engineer with sql skills

We Completely avoided routine data manipulation and data validation operation automating the ETL process using alteryx

We avoided dependency relations along the waterfall. This is another advantage to remove one step of the original structure and Having one unique professional that can cover the end to end.

Augmented rates of project delivered on time

Improved Graphics tenks to the usage of Tableau instead of excel for data visualisation;

Improved Data Quality which results in a major reliability on data from both point of view: Analytics department and client.

Allowance to develop 2 more reports per month per division

We Re-trained the whole analytics team in new software and project management skills to be kept within the organisation .

We Allowed Potentially the acquisition of one or more client.
