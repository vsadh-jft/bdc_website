Training

We don’t just teach software skills. We train aspiring consultants with an innovative approach to Business Intelligence and Data Analysis. This approach allows the aspirants to amass both, project management as well as core technical skills that enables them to deliver real value in a corporate environment as a BI Consultant that is certified in Tableau & Alteryx.

Our Approach

We start off by analysing a real-life case study and the request we received from the business manager. By giving a real-life scenario, we remove fiction from the process and can teach our aspirants the most crucial part of project management - How to add value to the project. This introduction to Project Management is extremely vital in giving aspirants the indispensable skills to engage at various level as a developer.

Once the aspirant has learned how to effectively manage a project and add value right at the very beginning of the process, they graduate to the next level where we train them in the ETL process and familiarise them with data architecture & provide the technical know-how required to manage databases and train them on softwares like ALTERYX.

Now that our aspirants have mastered the art of Project Management & ETL, we train them on data structuring and how to validate it. We teach them the crucial skills required for data analysis and educate them on effectively using Tableau as a visualisation tool.

Our training module and curriculum is amongst the best in the industry as it is developed by leading professionals in the field of Business Intelligence & Data Analytics. With years of experience in the corporate world, our trainers are the best guides and mentors you can find in the field of Business Intelligence. Don’t just take our word for it. Our training solutions has been trusted by PACKT publishers.
