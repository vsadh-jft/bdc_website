FABIO FIERRO

Fabio is the Director & the Principal Consultant at Business D Consulting. With over 5 years of experience in the industry, he has a steady record of accomplishment in delivering successful data management and BI solutions to major public, private and international organisations. His experience in Data Systems, coupled with a Master’s Degree in Economics & Management, gives him an edge in understanding the business objectives, looking at the bigger picture and creating change at the organisational level. List of clients that Fabio has worked with includes names like Ebay, National Grid, MRM Meteorite, Vauxhall, Opel, EasyJet, Lloyds Bank and Canon.

A closet chef with an Italian heritage backing him up, he is a professional dancer and an enthusiastic globetrotter. It is said, if you cannot find him in the office, try the nearest theatre!
